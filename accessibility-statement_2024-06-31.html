We take our commitment to our customers very seriously. We do our best this shows in our accessibility measures. Find out more in our statement.

Accessibility Statement for Satu

This is an accessibility statement from Satu Bookstore Oy.

Measures to support accessibility

Satu Bookstore Oy takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of Satu:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement.
  • Include accessibility throughout our internal policies.
  • Integrate accessibility into our procurement practices.
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff.
  • Assign clear accessibility goals and responsibilities.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. Satu is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Additional accessibility considerations

Although our goal is WCAG 2.1 Level AA conformance, we have also applied some Level AAA Success Criteria: No component flashes more than three times in any one second period. Web content does not restrict use of input modalities available on a platform. Information about the user's location within a set of Web pages is available. All functionality of the content is operable through a keyboard interface without requiring specific timings for individual keystrokes.

Compatibility with browsers and assistive technology

Satu is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Browser Firefox with assistive technology NVDA on operating system Windows 11
  • Browser Chrome with assistive technology NVDA on operating system Windows 11
  • Browser Firefox with assistive technology Narrator on operating system Windows 11
  • Browser Chrome with assistive technology Narrator on operating system Windows 11

Satu is not compatible with:

  • Browsers older than 3 major versions

Technical specifications

Accessibility of Satu relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.

Limitations and alternatives

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of Satu , there may be some limitations. Below is a description of known limitations, and potential solutions. Please contact us if you observe an issue not listed below.

Known limitations for Satu:

  1. Form error identification: Screen reader may not identify errors made in input fields because We do not provide custom validation scripts, but rely on browser's HTML5 support. We also accept verbal submissions by phone and can provide physical forms on premises. Please call us if you encounter an issue.
