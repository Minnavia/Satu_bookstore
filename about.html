About us

Satu is a reader's haven in the middle of Helsinki. Here you can enjoy delicious treats as you browse our collection. You might even find new bookish friends through our events. Welcome!

Satu was founded by two sisters from Kotka. They had always been close and had similar interests in books. Starting a book store together came naturally. Their respective degrees in library management and information technology helped their business succeed. Neither had any experience with entrepreneurship, but passion and hard work has taken them far.
