Events

We bring the book community together. We hold book clubs, themed parties and author interviews throughout the year. Hope to see you soon!

July 22 - August 12

Customer survey

To prepare for our 5th anniversary, we will be conducting a customer survey. Three of the participants will win a 50-euro gift card to Satu. Fill out the survey here.

Saturday 10.08. 6 p.m.

Finnish classics -book club

Finnish classics -bookclub returns this autumn to discuss Aleksis Kivi's foundational novel Seven brothers.

Monday 02.09. 5 p.m.

Meet the Author: Iida Turpeinen

In October we invite you to follow an interview with author Iida Turpeinen. Last year she made waves with her debut novel Elolliset (2023). There will be questions and answers, as well as an open discussion on topics such as natural history and the extinction of species.

Monday 07.01. 1 p.m.

Nevermoor-night

Readers young and old are welcomed to spend an evening inspired by Jessica Townsend's beloved Nevermoor-series! We promise all manner of wonderful games and activities for fans of all ages. Participants dressed according to the theme will be given a suprise gift!
